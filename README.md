## рҹ—ӮпёҸ Domain Deep Dives

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹҹЈ Supervised Learning</strong> вҖ” Learning from labeled data</summary>

<br/>

The model learns from input-output pairs. Given labeled training data, it maps inputs to correct outputs and generalizes to unseen examples.

**Regression** вҖ” predicts continuous values: price, temperature, salary, probability scores.
**Classification** вҖ” predicts discrete categories: spam/not spam, disease/healthy, cat/dog.

**Real-World:** Gmail spam filter (100B+ emails/day), Zillow Zestimate, Google DeepMind diabetic retinopathy screening, American Express credit risk scoring.

**Algorithms Covered:** Linear / Ridge / Lasso / ElasticNet Regression, Logistic Regression, KNN, Naive Bayes, Decision Tree, Random Forest, SVM, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost, LightGBM, CatBoost, Neural Network Classifier

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹҹЈ Unsupervised Learning</strong> вҖ” Discovering hidden structure</summary>

<br/>

The model receives unlabeled data and must find structure, patterns, or groupings on its own вҖ” no ground truth provided.

**Real-World:** Airbnb neighborhood segmentation, Mastercard fraud detection, single-cell RNA sequencing, Amazon market basket analysis.

**Algorithms Covered:** K-Means, Hierarchical Clustering, DBSCAN, Mean Shift, Gaussian Mixture Models, Spectral Clustering, PCA, t-SNE, UMAP, LDA, Autoencoders, Isolation Forest, One-Class SVM, Local Outlier Factor, Apriori, FP-Growth, Eclat

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹ”ө Semi-Supervised Learning</strong> вҖ” The best of both worlds</summary>

<br/>

Trains on a small amount of labeled data combined with a large pool of unlabeled data. Bridges the gap between supervised and unsupervised learning вҖ” especially powerful when labeling is expensive.

**Methods Covered:** Self-Training, Label Propagation, Label Spreading, Pseudo Labeling, Semi-Supervised Deep Learning

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹҹ  Reinforcement Learning</strong> вҖ” Learning through interaction</summary>

<br/>

An agent observes the environment, takes actions, receives rewards or penalties, and learns a policy that maximizes long-term cumulative reward.

**Real-World:** DeepMind AlphaGo / AlphaZero, OpenAI ChatGPT RLHF, Boston Dynamics locomotion, Google data center cooling (40% energy saved), Jane Street algorithmic trading.

**Algorithms Covered:** Q-Learning, DQN, Double DQN, Policy Gradient, REINFORCE, Actor-Critic, PPO, A3C, DDPG, SAC, Multi-Agent RL

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹҹў Deep Learning</strong> вҖ” Neural networks at scale</summary>

<br/>

Multi-layer neural networks that automatically learn hierarchical feature representations from raw data вҖ” images, text, audio, video вҖ” without manual feature engineering.

**Real-World:** OpenAI Whisper, AlphaFold2 protein folding, Stable Diffusion, Suno AI music generation, DeepMind GraphCast weather forecasting.

**Architectures Covered:** Perceptron, MLP, Feedforward NN, CNN, ResNet, VGG, Inception, EfficientNet, YOLO, Faster R-CNN, Vision Transformer, RNN, LSTM, GRU, Transformer, Autoencoders, VAE, GANs, Diffusion Models, LLMs, Pretrained Models, Fine-Tuning, Foundation Models

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹҹЎ Natural Language Processing</strong> вҖ” Making machines understand language</summary>

<br/>

NLP enables machines to read, interpret, generate, and act on human language вҖ” spanning classic text processing to modern generative AI.

**Real-World:** Google Search (BERT since 2019), Reuters automated news writing, DeepL translation, Zendesk AI customer support, Bloomberg GPT.

**Topics Covered:** Tokenization, Stopword Removal, Stemming, Lemmatization, Bag of Words, TF-IDF, Word2Vec, GloVe, FastText, Embeddings, Sentiment Analysis, NER, Machine Translation, QA, Summarization, Chatbots, Transformers, BERT, GPT, T5, LLaMA, RAG

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹ”ҙ Computer Vision</strong> вҖ” Teaching machines to see</summary>

<br/>

Computer Vision builds systems that interpret and reason about visual data вҖ” images, video, depth maps, and more.

**Real-World:** Paige.AI cancer pathology, Foxconn Apple supply chain inspection, John Deere crop disease detection, Mobileye ADAS in 800M+ vehicles, Face ID on 1B+ iPhones.

**Topics Covered:** Image Classification, Object Detection, Image Segmentation, Face Recognition, OCR, Pose Estimation, Image Generation, Video Analysis

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹ”ө Time Series ML</strong> вҖ” Learning from temporal patterns</summary>

<br/>

Time Series ML handles data with a time dimension вҖ” stock prices, sensor readings, weather patterns, user activity streams.

**Real-World:** Two Sigma / Renaissance Technologies quant trading, National Grid electricity forecasting, GE Predix predictive maintenance, Philips ICU early warning systems.

**Methods Covered:** Time Series Forecasting, ARIMA, SARIMA, Prophet, LSTM Forecasting, Temporal Convolutional Networks, Transformer Forecasting, Anomaly Detection in Time Series

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹҹЈ Recommendation Systems</strong> вҖ” Surfacing the right content</summary>

<br/>

Recommendation engines power Netflix (80% of views), Amazon (35% of revenue), Spotify (2.3B streams/week), TikTok, and YouTube.

**Methods Covered:** Content-Based Filtering, Collaborative Filtering, Matrix Factorization, Hybrid Recommendation, Neural Recommendation Systems, Ranking Models

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹҹў Model Evaluation</strong> вҖ” Knowing if your model actually works</summary>

<br/>

Evaluation connects model training to real-world performance. It ensures your model generalizes вҖ” not just memorizes.

**Real-World:** FDA AI device approval requires AUC thresholds, FICO score model validation, Basel III banking model risk management.

**Metrics Covered:** Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1-Score, Confusion Matrix, ROC-AUC, Log Loss, MAE, MSE, RMSE, RВІ, MAPE, Silhouette Score, Davies-Bouldin Index, Inertia

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹ”ҙ Feature Engineering</strong> вҖ” Turning raw data into signal</summary>

<br/>

Feature Engineering is often the highest-leverage step in the ML pipeline. Well-crafted features can outperform complex models trained on poorly prepared data.

**Real-World:** Uber surge pricing features, Epic Systems EHR pipelines, Wayfair furniture recommendation features, airline no-show probability.

**Techniques Covered:** Handling Missing Values, Label / One-Hot / Target Encoding, Standardization, Normalization, Robust Scaling, Feature Selection, Feature Extraction, Outlier Handling, Data Transformation

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹ”ө Model Optimization</strong> вҖ” Squeezing out every bit of performance</summary>

<br/>

Optimization spans hyperparameter search, generalization techniques, ensemble methods, and making models leaner for deployment.

**Real-World:** Apple Neural Engine optimization, DistilBERT (60% smaller, 97% BERT accuracy), Google Search model distillation, Mobileye INT8 ADAS inference.

**Topics Covered:** Grid Search, Random Search, Bayesian Optimization, Optuna, L1/L2 Regularization, Dropout, Cross Validation, Early Stopping, Bagging, Boosting, Stacking, Quantization, Pruning, Knowledge Distillation

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>рҹ©ө MLOps</strong> вҖ” Taking models from notebooks to production</summary>

<br/>

## рҹ“– Quick-Reference Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Transformer** | Self-attention + positional encoding + feed-forward layers вҖ” processes entire sequences in parallel. The backbone of GPT, BERT, DALLВ·E, and every major AI product since 2020. |
| **Dropout** | Randomly zeroes activations during training вҖ” reduces co-adaptation between neurons, approximates ensembling. Introduced by Hinton in 2012, now in virtually every deep network. |
| **DBSCAN** | Density-Based Spatial Clustering вҖ” groups dense regions, labels sparse outliers as noise. No need to specify K upfront. |
| **RAG** | Retrieval-Augmented Generation вҖ” retrieves relevant documents at inference time and conditions generation on them. Reduces hallucination and keeps models current. |
| **PPO** | Proximal Policy Optimization вҖ” clips the policy ratio to limit update size, making RL training stable and sample-efficient. The default algorithm for RLHF fine-tuning of LLMs. |
| **t-SNE** | Converts pairwise similarities into probabilities and minimizes KL divergence in low-dimensional space. The gold standard for visualizing high-dimensional embeddings. |
| **Diffusion Models** | Learn to reverse a Gaussian noising process step-by-step вҖ” state-of-the-art for image, audio, and video generation. |
| **Knowledge Distillation** | Small student network trained to match soft logits of a large teacher. The technology behind compact, deployable on-device AI. |
| **Embeddings** | Continuous dense vector representations where geometric relationships encode semantic and syntactic similarity. |
| **Matrix Factorization** | Decomposes the user-item matrix into low-rank latent factors вҖ” won the Netflix Prize ($1M) in 2009 and powers modern recommendation systems. |
